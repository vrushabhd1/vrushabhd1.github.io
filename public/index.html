About Me

I build AI, systems & control, and optimization-driven solutions for grid modernization and power-electronics reliability—bridging advanced ML/control research with real-world energy workflows.

My research spans reinforcement learning, health monitoring (diagnostics & prognostics), multi-agent systems, and intelligent control of dynamic energy systems.

What I bring

  • Translating ambiguous grid/asset problems into clear product requirements and success metrics
  • Designing “minimum lovable” prototypes and iterating based on performance, reliability, and user needs
  • Deep domain context across distribution systems, DER integration, and power-electronics asset health
  • Strong technical foundation in AI/ML, reinforcement learning, optimization/MPC, and time-series modeling

Interests

I’m especially interested in building products for utilities, grid software vendors, and energy-tech teams focused on:

  • Grid analytics and DERMS/ADMS workflows
  • Predictive maintenance and asset-health intelligence
  • Reliable, scalable optimization + control in operational settings
